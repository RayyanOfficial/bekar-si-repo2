At Aplex Punch., we convert raster images into high-quality vector artwork. Converting a raster image to a vector requires precision and expertise. The demand for vector images continues to grow because they offer exceptional flexibility in terms of resolution. This makes vector artwork an ideal choice for both print and digital media industries. Here are some important factors to consider when converting raster images to vector artwork. The main formats suitable for vector graphics include AI, EPS, PDF, SVG, CDR, and DXF.
